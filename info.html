How are stolen bikes reported?

Buying a stolen bicycle is a crime encourages bike theft. This tool lets you check a bicycle’s serial number against the Canada-wide CPIC police database to see if it has been reported as stolen. If you suspect a bicycle is stolen but the serial number doesn’t show up positive, this could be due to a number of reasons:

  • The theft could not have been reported.
  • There is a delay from when a theft is reported to the serial number being added to the database.
  • You could have entered the serial number incorrectly or missed a number.

Remember: if the price is too good to be true, it probably is stolen.
